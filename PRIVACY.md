QuoteLight Chrome Extension - Privacy Policy

# QuoteLight — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 28, 2026

## Overview

QuoteLight is a Chrome extension that lets you highlight text on any webpage and share it to social media with article attribution. It is designed with privacy as a core principle.

## Data Collection

QuoteLight does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — No usage data, page visits, or user behavior is recorded.
- **No external servers** — QuoteLight does not communicate with any external server. All functionality runs entirely within your browser.
- **No accounts** — No sign-up, login, or authentication is required.

## Data Storage

The only data QuoteLight stores is:

- **Mastodon instance URL** — If you choose to share to Mastodon, QuoteLight saves your instance URL (e.g., "mastodon.social") locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared at any time by removing the extension.

No other data is stored, cached, or persisted.

## Page Content Access

QuoteLight reads page content (meta tags, JSON-LD markup, page title) solely to extract article metadata (title, author, publication name) for attribution in your shares. This information is:

- Read locally in your browser
- Used only at the moment you make a selection
- Never transmitted, logged, or stored

## Sharing

When you click a share button, QuoteLight opens the selected platform's standard share URL in a new tab. Your highlighted text and article metadata are passed via URL parameters directly to the platform. QuoteLight does not act as an intermediary — the data goes straight from your browser to the platform you chose.

## Third Parties

QuoteLight does not use any third-party services, SDKs, APIs, or analytics tools.
